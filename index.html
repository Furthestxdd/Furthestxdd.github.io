Disney's Ratatouille

67,781 Likes

By Commander M.


Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Remove from heat.
  3. Add cooked spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat with garlic oil. Stir in Parmesan cheese and parsley. Serve immediately.
